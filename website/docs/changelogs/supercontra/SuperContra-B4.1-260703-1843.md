### New Features
🎶 New Soundscapes widgets are now available, offering curated ambient audio to help you find your flow: focus, relaxation, or sleep.

📶 Dual network signals are now integrated into a single status bar icon for a cleaner look.  

### Essential Voice
Essential Voice, a new AI-powered speech-to-text feature, lets you focus on your ideas efficiently. When using the keyboard, start it by tapping the icon, or by long-pressing or double-clicking the Essential key. Key features include:  
🎙️ Cleans up transcriptions by filtering out interjections, filler words, and repetitions, while intelligently handling your self-corrections.  
🔡 Translates on the fly by detecting your translation intent and inserting the translated text directly.  
🗃️ Allows you to store personal phrases, like email addresses, and automatically inserts them when triggered by corresponding speech.  
📑 Formats the text automatically by converting speech into structured lists, steps, or bullet points when identifying those contents from the speaker.  
⌨️ Supports quickly switching between translation and transcription by long-pressing the icon on the keyboard.  

#### Notes
For more details about Essential Voice, please go to Settings > Intelligence toolkit > Essential Voice.
