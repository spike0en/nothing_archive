# Nothing Archive

Your ultimate source for Nothing OS firmware, stock OTA images, community apps, projects, resources, detailed guides and more for Nothing & CMF by Nothing devices.

<div class="features-grid">
  <div class="feature-card">
    <div class="feature-icon">📦</div>
    <div class="feature-title">Factory Images</div>
    <div class="feature-desc">Provides flashable system packages generated for every software build. This is the only source on the open web, offering an alternative to Google's factory images.</div>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <div class="feature-icon">⚡</div>
    <div class="feature-title">Delta OTA Updates</div>
    <div class="feature-desc">Indexes incremental delta OTA packages for immediate manual sideloading. This lets you bypass slow, staggered regional rollouts.</div>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <div class="feature-icon">📱</div>
    <div class="feature-title">Device Catalog</div>
    <div class="feature-desc">Lists Nothing and CMF devices with their model numbers, specifications, release dates, and codenames.</div>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <div class="feature-icon">📖</div>
    <div class="feature-title">Technical Guides</div>
    <div class="feature-desc">Provides step-by-step guides for bootloader unlocking, flashing, rooting, partition backups, and troubleshooting.</div>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <div class="feature-icon">📜</div>
    <div class="feature-title">Software Changelogs</div>
    <div class="feature-desc">Indexes full release changelogs, system updates, and security patches for every model since its release date.</div>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <div class="feature-icon">🧩</div>
    <div class="feature-title">Community Index</div>
    <div class="feature-desc">Indexes community-built apps, tools, and developer projects to make them easier to find.</div>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <div class="feature-icon">⚙️</div>
    <div class="feature-title">Aftermarket Projects</div>
    <div class="feature-desc">Categorizes custom ROMs, kernels, recoveries, and root modules for aftermarket development.</div>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <div class="feature-icon">📸</div>
    <div class="feature-title">Photography Resources</div>
    <div class="feature-desc">Lists GCam ports, camera configs, assets, and custom LUTs to improve mobile photography.</div>
  </div>
</div>
